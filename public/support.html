Common questions

How does AegisDial get my call audio?

It doesn't. Live Shield uses Apple's on-device speech recognizer. No audio ever leaves your phone.

Why does the shield need microphone permission?

To transcribe the call on-device. Without the mic permission, the shield can't detect scam phrases.

Does AegisDial work during a phone call?

Yes, when you tap "Shield this call" while the phone is on speakerphone or connected to a Bluetooth/wired headset. iOS doesn't let third-party apps tap call audio directly — the shield listens through your device's microphone.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Open the iPhone Settings app → tap your Apple ID at the top → Subscriptions → AegisDial → Cancel.

I got flagged but I'm sure it's legit

In the app, tap "Report" on the verdict card and choose "Not a scam." Your feedback tunes the engine for everyone.

How do I add a family member?

Settings → Family Plan → Invite. Share the 8-character code with them; they enter it in their copy of AegisDial.

My safe-word verify keeps failing — I know it's right

Safe words are case-insensitive and whitespace-normalized. If it still fails, you may have mis-typed when you saved it. Settings → Deepfake Defense → Family Contacts → edit the contact → Change safe word.

File a complaint
